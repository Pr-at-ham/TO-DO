Time Blocking

Allocate specific blocks of time to different tasks or categories of work. This technique involves creating a schedule where you focus on one type of activity during each designated time block. It helps in maintaining concentration by minimizing multitasking and providing dedicated periods for specific tasks.

The Eisenhower Matrix

Prioritize tasks using the Eisenhower Matrix, which categorizes activities into four quadrants based on urgency and importance. This method helps in focusing on high-priority tasks and avoiding time-wasting activities, contributing to increased productivity and better decision-making.

The 2-Minute Rule

If a task takes less than two minutes to complete, do it immediately. This technique, popularized by productivity expert David Allen, is effective for tackling small tasks promptly, preventing them from accumulating and becoming overwhelming. It streamlines decision-making and keeps productivity high throughout the day.
